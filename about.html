Get to Know Me

Hallo! I'm Paul Meisner, a tech enthusiast born and raised in Germany. My fascination with technology ignited at a young age, propelling me into the captivating world of coding and software engineering. Following my education, I delved into the realm of Software Engineering and Information Systems at the University of Eau Claire in Wisconsin, driven by a thirst for knowledge and a desire to make a meaningful impact in the tech industry. Throughout my journey, I've had the privilege of collaborating on diverse projects, further fueling my passion for innovation and problem-solving. Constantly pushing boundaries, I'm dedicated to exploring new possibilities and embracing every opportunity for growth. Beyond the digital landscape, I enjoy venturing into the great outdoors, discovering new destinations, and indulging in quiet moments with a good book and a cup of coffee.
